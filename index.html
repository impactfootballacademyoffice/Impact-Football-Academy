Our Story

MORE THAN A CLUB

Impact Football Academy was born from a single belief — that every player, regardless of age or background, deserves world-class coaching and the chance to reach their true potential on the pitch.

From grassroots juniors to competitive adults, we build more than footballers. We build discipline, resilience, teamwork, and an unshakeable love for the beautiful game.

Based in Aurangabad, Maharashtra, our academy has produced players who have gone on to represent at district, state, and national levels.
